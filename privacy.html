01
Introduction

Snozz ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application that helps commuters wake up when their public transport approaches their chosen stop. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal data when you use the Snozz app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the Snozz service, and we never sell your data to third parties.

Plain English summary: Snozz needs your location to wake you up at your stop. We store your journey history on your device. We do not track you, we do not sell your data, and we do not share your location with anyone.

This policy complies with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and Apple App Store requirements.

02
What Data We Collect

Snozz collects only the data necessary to provide the stop alarm service.

Journey History Stop name, line, date, time of journeys To show your history and streak count On your device only

03
How We Use Your Location

Location access is the core of the Snozz service. Here is exactly how we use it:

Foreground location — used when you set your stop and begin a journey, to show your position on the route.

Background location (Always) — used while a journey is active to monitor your GPS position and trigger the wake alarm when you are within 400 metres of your chosen stop. This is the only reason we request "Always" location access.

Background location is only active when you have started a journey. When you dismiss the alarm or cancel the journey, location tracking stops immediately. We never monitor your location outside of an active journey.

Your GPS coordinates are processed entirely on your device. They are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

You can revoke location permissions at any time in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Snozz. If you revoke background location, the stop alarm will not function while your screen is locked.

04
Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) — You explicitly grant location permission when you first use Snozz. You can withdraw this consent at any time via iPhone Settings.

Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)) — Processing your location data is necessary to deliver the core stop alarm service you have requested.

Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — Storing journey history and preferences on your device to provide a functional, personalised experience.

05
Data Storage & Retention

All Snozz data is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's secure AsyncStorage system. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Journey history — stored on your device, retained until you delete the app or clear app data.

Location data — never stored. GPS coordinates are processed in real time and discarded immediately after comparison with your target stop.

Deleting your data — you can delete all Snozz data by deleting the app from your iPhone. All locally stored data is permanently removed.
